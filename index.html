top 3 jobs

Tree Top provides job opportunities for growth with competitive salaries and benefits in an exciting, fast-paced workplace. We value the unique set of experiences, perspectives and background that each person brings to our business. We are looking for talented and energetic individuals who want to join our team and join our vision of being the first choice fruit-based company.

At Tree Top, we produce world famous Tree Top brand juices and the widest range of processed fruit products of superior quality. Headquartered in the rural community of Selah, Washington, in the heart of Washington’s apple country, we have held true to our small town heritage of care, craftsmanship, and quality since 1960. We are an agricultural cooperative owned by almost 1,000 apple and pear growers in the Northwestern United States.

With the acquisition of Northwest Naturals, a wholly-owned subsidiary, and the Sabroso Company, the Co-op owns and operates eight production facilities throughout the West in close proximity to the fruit of our growers. Our plants are located in Selah, Prosser and Wenatchee, Washington; Medford and Woodburn, Oregon; and Oxnard, California.
